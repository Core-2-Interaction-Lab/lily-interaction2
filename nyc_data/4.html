RISK OF INFECTION:
Dog bites can lead to bacterial infections such as tetanus, rabies, and Pasteurella.
PHYSICAL INJURIES:
Dog bites can cause physical injuries ranging from minor puncture wounds to severe injuries such as fractures and disfiguration.
EMOTIONAL TRAUMA:
Dog bites can also cause emotional trauma and fear, particularly in children who may develop a fear of dogs after being bitten.
FINANCIAL COSTS:
The medical treatment required for dog bite injuries can be costly, particularly for severe injuries that require hospitalization and surgery.
